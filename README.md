# 📊 Trader Behavior vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## 🚀 Overview
This project analyzes how market sentiment (Fear vs Greed) impacts trader behavior and performance using historical trading data.

## 🎯 Objective
To uncover patterns in trading activity and profitability across different market sentiment regimes and generate actionable trading strategies.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Trader data (Hyperliquid)
- Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

---

## 🧠 Key Features Engineered
- Daily PnL per trader
- Win rate
- Trade frequency
- Average position size (risk proxy)
- Long/Short behavior

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 1. Extreme Greed Drives Maximum Profitability
- PnL is 3–5x higher compared to normal market conditions
- Win rate peaks (~55%)

### 2. Fear Leads to Overtrading & Inefficiency
- Trade frequency increases ~2–3x
- Win rate drops significantly

### 3. Medium Risk Traders Perform Best
- Medium-risk segment outperforms high-risk by ~2x

---

## ⚙️ Trading Strategies

### Strategy 1 — Sentiment-Adaptive Trading
- Increase position size during Extreme Greed
- Reduce exposure during Fear

### Strategy 2 — Optimal Risk Allocation
- Avoid extreme risk levels
- Maintain moderate position sizes

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
Strategies are based on historical analysis and should be validated before real-world deployment.
